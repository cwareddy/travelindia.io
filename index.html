About Us

TravelAdvisor was founded by Stephen Kaufer, Langley Steinert, Nick Shanny, and Thomas Palka in February 2000.[13] Kaufer has stated that the original idea wasn't a user generated social media site to swap reviews, "We started as a site where we were focused more on those official words from guidebooks or newspapers or magazines. We also had a button in the very beginning that said, "Visitors add your own review", and boy, did that just take off."[14] Original financing was obtained from Flagship Ventures, the Bollard Group, and private investors.[15]

In 2004, the company was purchased by IAC/InterActiveCorp.[3] In August 2005, IAC spun off its travel group of businesses under the Expedia, Inc. name.[16] Headquarters in Needham, Massachusetts In April 2009, TripAdvisor launched its official site in China, since then it has indexed more than 20,000 hotels and restaurants information and customer reviews, and made top lists, becoming one of the biggest travel websites as of July 2011.[17] In September 2010, SmarterTravel, part of TripAdvisor Media Group, launched SniqueAway (now Jetsetter), the first members-only site where each travel deal is endorsed by the people.[18]
